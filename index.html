Who I Am

I'm Surabhi — a Mumbai-based Senior Model Validation Analyst at Deutsche India and a canvas painter with a deep love for art. I graduated from IIT Jodhpur with a B.Tech in Materials Engineering, finishing third in my department with a CGPA of 8.50, where I discovered that the same analytical precision that builds a good risk model also creates a compelling painting composition.

At Deutsche Bank, I work in the Model Risk Management (MoRM) team — independently validating financial models across CCAR regulatory cycles spanning Credit Risk, Market Risk, and PPNR. I work with Python and R daily, write validation findings, and build challenger models to stress-test assumptions. It's rigorous, detail-driven work that I genuinely love.

My Story as an Artist

Painting is my parallel universe — one where there are no confidence intervals or regulatory findings, just colour, instinct, and feeling. I had the honour of representing IIT Jodhpur at the InterIIT Cultural Meet Canvas Painting Competition at both IIT Madras (2023) and IIT Kharagpur (2024). I also served as Joint Secretary of FRAMEX, IIT Jodhpur's video-editing and filmmaking society. I believe great art, like good risk modelling, is about seeing the patterns others miss.
